RUNTOWN-FOR LIFE

For Life" is a romantic afrobeats song released by Nigerian artist Runtown in 2016. The song features smooth, melodic vocals over a mellow afrobeats production, where Runtown expresses deep love and commitment to his romantic interest. With its catchy chorus and romantic lyrics, it became one of Runtown's signature hits and a popular choice for weddings and romantic occasions across Africa.

RUNTOWN-ENERGY

"Energy" is Runtown's 2018 chart-topping hit blending Afrobeat rhythms with smooth vocals. The track became a continental anthem, celebrated for its infectious melody and relatable lyrics about love and positive vibes.


OMAY LAY-SOSO

>"Soso" is a song released by Nigerian singer Omah Lay in 2022. It's a melodic afrobeats track that showcases his signature emotional vocal style. The song deals with themes of heartbreak and relationship struggles, delivered with Omah Lay's characteristic blend of afrobeats and contemporary R&B elements. It was released as part of his debut album "Boy Alone" and received positive reception for its production quality and emotional depth.



OMAY LAY-HOLY GHOST

Holy Ghost is a song released by Nigerian artist Omah Lay in 2023. It's an afrobeats track that showcases his signature melodic style, blending contemporary afrobeats with R&B elements. The song explores themes of spirituality and relationships, using religious metaphors. It was released as a single and received positive reception for its production quality and Omah Lay's vocal delivery.
